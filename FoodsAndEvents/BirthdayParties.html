Birthday Parties

Celebrate your birthday with a tenpin bowling party @ Planet Tenpin

Bowling parties start at $19 per person this includes one game, shoe hire and your choice of one meal option for each party guest. Each party guest can choose one meal option. Drinks can be a small fizzy drink, popper juice or water.

    Menu
  • Nuggets with chips + drink
  • Hotdog with fries + drink
  • Cheese burger with fries + drink

    • Conditions: Minimum of 10 guests. To confirm a booking a minimum 20% non-refundable deposit is required.

    Celebrate your birthday in the Kids Planet

    Playground parties for children up to age 10. Kids Planet parties start at $18 per child which includes entry into the play area, party food and drink. Each party can choose 2 party platter food items and each party guest receives 1 drink – popper juice or small soft drink. Jugs of water are provided free of charge. Additional food platter items available @ $2 extra per party guest.

      Food choices:
    • Fairy bread
    • Fruit
    • Hot dogs
    • Mixed sandwiches
    • Nuggets with chips
    • Party pies/sausage rolls
    • Adult meal options – $10/person
    • Schnitzel burger with chips
    • Cheese burger with fries
    • Hot dog with fries
