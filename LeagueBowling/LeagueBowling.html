League Bowling

There are tenpin bowling leagues to suit everyone.
We have leagues for children, adults, seniors and also people with a
disability and we offer free learn to bowl sessions if you need a few
tips to get you started.

You can get a group of family and friends together and form your own
team or we can find a team that is looking for a bowler. League bowling
takes place on Tuesday, Wednesday & Thursday nights, Thursday mornings
and Saturday afternoons. Our leagues compete for trophies or prize
money and celebrate at the end of season with awards parties.

Meet new people, get some light exercise, friendly competition & prize
money. All while having fun.

What are you waiting for? Give us a call today and get into league bowling.
